Why Small Group

The Best of Both Worlds

Private training gives you attention. Team practice gives you competition. Small group training gives you both.

With a cap of 4 players, every rep still matters and sessions are still built around individual development. But the presence of serious players adds live pressure, competitive repetition, and a standard that only comes from training alongside others who are working just as hard.

Sessions are structured, focused, and demanding. No filler. No standing in line.

Individual Attention
Max 4 players — your technique is still coached in real time, not lost in a crowd.
Live Competition
Rondos, 1v1s, and possession games at full speed. The group creates pressure solo work can't.
Shared Standard
Every player in the group is serious. The environment reflects that — and it raises everyone.
Cost-Effective
High-quality technical coaching at a lower per-session cost than private training.

Session Focus

What We Work On

01
Technical Reps Under Pressure
First touch, passing combinations, and receiving mechanics executed at pace — with defenders, time pressure, and competitive consequence.
02
Possession & Pressing
Rondos and small-sided games that build the ability to keep the ball, break pressure, and win it back intelligently.
03
1v1 & 2v2 Scenarios
Attacking and defending in tight spaces. Decision-making, body positioning, and executing under live defensive pressure.
04
Position-Specific Detail
Sessions are adapted to the players in the group. Role-specific coaching for wingers, midfielders, forwards, and defenders.
