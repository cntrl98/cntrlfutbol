About CNTRL Futbol

Built From
Experience.

CNTRL was founded by a former professional player and NJ native who has trained and competed alongside players now representing the United States on the world stage. The standard at that level was never loud. It was consistent, detail-oriented, and demanding. That's what we bring to every session.

Kyle Gurrieri
Founder & Head Coach

Kyle is a New Jersey native and former professional player with experience at the highest levels of the American development pathway.

He competed at the U.S. Youth National Team level from U14 through U17, training and playing alongside athletes who now represent the United States on the world stage. That environment set the benchmark early — elite performance is built on discipline, tactical intelligence, and daily technical refinement.

He continued his career at the NCAA Division I level before earning the opportunity to train and compete professionally in both the United States and Sweden. Experiencing the full progression from youth soccer to college to the national team to the professional game provided firsthand insight into what truly separates players as they advance.

CNTRL is built on that experience. Built on accountability. Built on standards.
