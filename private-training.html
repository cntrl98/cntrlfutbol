Session Focus

What We Work On

01
First Touch
Receiving under pressure. Clean control in tight spaces. The foundation everything else is built on — drilled until it's automatic.
02
Decision Making
Reading the game. Playing at pace with composure. Knowing what to do before the ball arrives — training the brain alongside the feet.
03
Technical Mechanics
Body shape. Weight of pass. Striking technique. The details that separate players who look good in training from those who perform in matches.
04
Position-Specific Detail
Training tailored to your role, your weaknesses, and the level you are trying to reach. No generic curriculum — every session built around you.

Who This Is For

Serious Players Only

Private training at CNTRL is for players who are ready to close the gap between where they are and where they want to be.

We work with players at every level — from young athletes building their foundation to high school and academy players preparing for the next stage. What every player has in common: they show up ready to work, take coaching, and are committed to consistent development.
